# Pending-delete

Files and content excerpts that have been removed from the Content Studio 6
documentation during the CS5 → CS6 restructure. Each file in this folder starts
with a note explaining **why it was dropped** and **where its editorial remnants
(if any) were folded in**.

Delete this folder once you've confirmed nothing here is needed.

## What ends up here

1. **Whole pages** that were superseded or merged into other pages
   (e.g. `widgets.adoc`, `page-editor.adoc`, `component-types.adoc`).
2. **Excerpts** pulled out of pages that were otherwise kept — schema/XML
   sections that belong in the XP CMS docs, not in Content Studio's editor-
   facing docs. These get a filename like `<original>-<section>.adoc`.

## Why files are in here rather than just deleted

The restructure is a judgement call. Putting files here lets you scan what
was removed and either confirm the removal, reclaim something useful, or
point out things I mis-categorized.
